The Gaucho Internet Speed Test

A project to offer any user free and accurate internet speed testing as well as information about potential improvements.

01

ANALYSIS TESTS

  • Click the buttons below to instantly assess your device's network speeds!


    DOWNLOAD SPEED
    Click this button to test your download speed, a measure of how fast your device can retrieve data from remote servers.

    UPLOAD SPEED
    Click this button to test your upload speed, a measure of how fast your device can send data to other servers.

    NETWORK LATENCY
    Click this button to test your network latency, or ping, the measured amount of time it takes your device to get a response after sending out a request.
